First Green

Golf to !!!Empower Underprivileged Youth

First Green is a youth development charity group that enables kids to have an opportunity to enjoy the game of golf and build the strength of character that empowers them through a lifetime of new challenges. By seamlessly integrating the game of golf with a life skills curriculum, we create active learning experiences that build inner strength, self-confidence, and resilience that kids can carry to everything they do. We're building game changers through our junior golf programs.
